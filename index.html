About Me

I’m Baikunth Nath, A UI/UX designer currently studying at Arena Animation Janakpuri, Delhi, with plans to complete in 2024. I'm interested in interaction design-the making of things intuitive and frictionless for users to interact with. Currently, I emphasize building bases in design principles, prototyping, wireframing, and user-centered design. I like how designers simplify complex ideas into engaging experiences. Along with my studies, I have six months experience in graphic design, which has helped me develop a versatile skill set. My Behance portfolio shows some of the work done so far. I try to keep the house between creativity and practicality while designing something. My focus is more toward designing something which will look good but always keeps at its center the user's experience about it.

UI/UX Designer

I am excited about designing user experiences that feel intuitive as well as engaging. As a UI/UX designer, I focus on understanding the needs of the user and how the aesthetics and functionality could be blended together while working on a project. I have put this into practice through practical projects where I have needed to wireframe, prototype, and usability test the design created in line with business goals and what the end-user would expect. I bring fresh eyes and perspectives to the table together with a solid grasp of design principles. Also, I am committed to continuous learning.
